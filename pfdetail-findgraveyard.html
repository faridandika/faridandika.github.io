Find Graveyard

Project Information

  • Output: Sketch Prototype
  • Project Date: August 2024
  • Project URL: -

Find Graveyard is a (solution concept) prototype iOS app that can help family to find the closest graveyard considering their religion/believe and their living location so they can bury bodies efficiently

Things I Learned in this Project:

  • Desk Research: Investigated challenges and needs across cultural and religious groups.
  • Interviews: Gathered insights from Bataknese, Chinese, and Javanese communities on burial practices.
  • Lotus Blossom Technique: Used to refine ideas for a more effective graveyard-finding solution.
  • Sketch Design: Created design sketches to ensure a user-friendly and accessible app interface.
